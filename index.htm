Printing Problems?

You may need to adjust your printing settings, particularly for stage size. Ensure that scaling is set to 100%. Also, you may need to turn off headers and footers, and adjust your margins.

It's easier to ensure everything is correct using your browser's print preview functionality. When you click the "Print" button above, some browsers will automatically show you a preview. Others won't. You don't have to use the print button. You can use your browser's menu to open print preview instead.

Browsers

Theoretically, this utility should work in modern browsers. Reality is never that simple. Given that this is a "quick and dirty" utility, there's a limit to how many hours I'm willing to spend resolving browser specific compatibility issues. (Hey, remember what you paid for this!)

Chrome

This utility was designed for Chrome. If you run into trouble with other browsers, you may want to try it in Chrome.

Firefox

Firefox seems to work fine, as long as your print settings are correct. I recommend opening Print Preview from the Firefox menu rather than using the print button, so you can be sure everything looks good before printing.

Microsoft Edge

Edge versions 18 and lower don't work. The upcoming version 79 likely will. (Really. 79 is the next version after 18.)

Internet Explorer

No. Just no.

Safari

I don't know, I'm not a Mac guy. Give it a shot and let me know how it goes.
